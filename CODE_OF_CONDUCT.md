# Code of Conduct

All participants in Saluca LLC projects — contributors, maintainers, issue reporters, commenters — are expected to engage professionally and respectfully.

## Standards

- Be direct and technical; criticism of code, design, or ideas is welcome.
- Personal attacks, harassment, and discriminatory conduct are not.
- Assume good faith; clarify before escalating.
- Confidential information (customer data, internal credentials, unpublished security findings) does not belong in public issues or PRs.

## Enforcement

Report violations to **conduct@saluca.com**. Maintainers will review and respond privately. Outcomes may include private correction, temporary or permanent removal from the project, or referral to legal counsel for harassment that crosses statutory lines.

## Scope

This code applies in all project spaces — repos, issue trackers, pull requests, discussions, chat channels, and in-person events where Saluca LLC is represented.
