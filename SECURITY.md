# Security Policy

## Reporting a vulnerability

If you discover a security vulnerability in any Saluca LLC product, **do not open a public issue**. Email **security@saluca.com** with:

- Affected product and version
- Steps to reproduce
- Impact assessment
- Your name and contact info for follow-up (if you want credit)

We aim to acknowledge reports within **2 business days** and produce a fix or mitigation timeline within **10 business days** for confirmed issues.

## Scope

In scope for coordinated disclosure:
- Anything published under the `salucallc` or `saluca-labs` GitHub organizations.
- Any Saluca-hosted service: `*.saluca.com`, `*.tiresias.network`, `*.asphodel.ai`.

Out of scope:
- Rate-limiting issues on public endpoints (report as a regular issue).
- Vulnerabilities in third-party dependencies that have not been verified as exploitable in our deployment.

## Safe-harbor

Security researchers acting in good faith under this policy will not be subject to legal action from Saluca LLC for the research described above.
